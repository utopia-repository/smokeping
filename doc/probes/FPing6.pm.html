NAME

probes::FPing6 - FPing6 Probe for SmokePing

SYNOPSIS

 *** Probes ***
 + FPing6
 binary = /usr/bin/fping6

DESCRIPTION

Integrates FPing6 as a probe into smokeping. The variable binary must point to your copy of the FPing6 program. If it is not installed on your system yet, you can get it from http://www.fping.com/.
