       probes::FPing6 - FPing6 Probe for SmokePing

DDEESSCCRRIIPPTTIIOONN
       Integrates FPing6 as a probe into smokeping. The variable
       bbiinnaarryy must point to your copy of the FPing6 program. If
       it is not installed on your system yet, you can get it
       from http://www.fping.com/.
